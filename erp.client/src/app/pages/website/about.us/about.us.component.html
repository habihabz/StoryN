🎮 What We Do

We design narrative-driven experiences where students don’t just learn — they investigate, argue, build, and solve. Each game is a fully online, standalone alternate reality simulation, carefully designed for specific disciplines and learning outcomes.

  • Set in a parallel world, students role-play as characters with a purpose.
  • Every decision impacts the narrative.
  • Tasks are aligned with academic outcomes but unfold through mystery, conflict, and collaboration.

Perfect For: Higher Education Institutions, Professional Training Programs, Workshops and Bootcamps
